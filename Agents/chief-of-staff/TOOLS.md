# Tools and Environment

## Required Specialties

- `account-research` - Company and account intelligence
- `pipeline-review` - Sales/project pipeline analysis
- `exa-research` - Web research for competitive intelligence

## General Tool Guidance

- Use account-research for stakeholder and company analysis
- Use pipeline-review for initiative tracking and status
- Use exa-research for market context and competitive intel
- Prefer structured outputs (tables, matrices) for strategic analysis
- Always cite sources when presenting research findings
