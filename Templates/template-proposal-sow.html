03

Scope of Work

This section defines the boundaries of the engagement. Items listed under "In Scope" are included in the pricing and timeline. Items under "Out of Scope" are excluded but can be addressed via a change order.

In Scope

  • Discovery & requirements gathering — Stakeholder interviews, process mapping, technical assessment
  • Solution design — Architecture, data model, integration blueprint, UI/UX wireframes
  • Development & configuration — Core platform build, API integrations, data migration
  • Testing & QA — Unit testing, integration testing, UAT support, performance validation
  • Training & documentation — Admin training, end-user guides, knowledge transfer sessions
  • Go-live support — 2-week hypercare period with dedicated support channel

Out of Scope

  • Third-party license fees (client responsibility)
  • Legacy data cleanup prior to migration
  • Ongoing managed services post-hypercare
  • Custom hardware procurement or infrastructure changes
Change Management
Any scope additions or changes after contract execution will be documented via a formal Change Order with associated impact to timeline and investment.

Approach & Methodology

We follow an agile delivery methodology with structured phase gates. This approach balances flexibility with predictability — allowing us to adapt to new insights while maintaining accountability to the agreed timeline and budget.

01
Discover — Deep dive into business context, constraints, and opportunities
02
Design — Architect the solution, validate with stakeholders, finalize blueprint
03
Develop — Build iteratively in sprints with weekly progress reviews
04
Validate — Rigorous QA, UAT, and performance testing
05
Launch — Production deployment with training and hypercare support
Communication Cadence
Weekly status reports (written), bi-weekly steering committee meetings, daily standups during active development sprints. A shared project channel in Slack for real-time coordination.
